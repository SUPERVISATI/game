📖 STORY

In a distant future, humanity faces an unknown threat: hostile energy spheres invading our planet. You are the last defender of Earth, commanding a protection barrier that keeps the invaders away.

Each level brings new challenges, powerful bosses, and increasingly greater rewards. Destroy enemies, collect coins, and prove you are the best warrior in the galaxy!

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

The goal is simple: destroy all targets before time runs out or your barrier is destroyed!

  • Click or tap on the screen to shoot enemies
  • Eliminate targets to earn coins and advance levels
  • Protect your barrier - if it's destroyed, the game ends
  • Collect power-ups to increase your firepower
  • Defeat the bosses to advance to the next level
🖱️ CONTROLS
🖱️ Click / Tap
Shoot enemies
🛡️ Barrier

🎯 Aim

🧠 STRATEGIES
  • Focus on smaller targets - eliminate them quickly to prevent barrier damage
  • Collect coins - use them to buy more powerful weapons in the shop
  • Watch out for bosses - they have special attacks and more health
  • Power-ups are essential - special balloons can turn the tide
  • Manage your time - each level has a 5-minute limit
  • Balance attack and defense - destroy enemies before they reach your barrier
